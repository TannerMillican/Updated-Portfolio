About Me

Hi! My name is Tanner Millican. I'm 24, married, and have three cats. I've had quite a few jobs, but my most recent one was as a POS field technician. I've always had a great fondness for computers and technology in general.

That fondness for technology is what has lead me to the world of coding and web development. Having been a POS technician, I was able to get some hands on experience actually getting to work with technology (computers and internet servers especially). But something was missing, I wasn't satisfied. And then I found the Coding Bootcamp at UT in Austin, Texas.

With the skills I'm learning, the doors are beginning to open for me into a world that satiates my hunger for creating along with fueling my drive to dive even further into the world of technology. Not only that, but it's given me a goal to shoot for in terms of a career. That goal is to hopefully one day work on the dev team for my favorite online MMO, Runescape.
